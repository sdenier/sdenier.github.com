Interest

I am curious about the art and craft of programming.

I have practiced object and aspect-oriented programming to figure out programming in the small. I am also interested in reverse engineering and reengineering of large programs. I worked on software assessment, software visualization, and change simulation. I am also a dedicated participant of Moose, a platform and a community for tools and collaboration in reengineering.

I believe that reengineering tools will become important in the day-to-day development process. And I am eagerly pushing such tools in programming environments.

Keywords: object-oriented programming, reverse engineering, reengineering, software visualization.
